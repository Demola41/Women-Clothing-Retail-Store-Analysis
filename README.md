## 1. Situation
Analyzed a dataset of **23,486 customer reviews** to evaluate product sentiment and recommendation patterns for a women's clothing e-commerce retailer.
## 2. Task
The goal was to identify top-performing departments, correlate age demographics with rating behavior, and pinpoint specific product classes driving positive feedback.
## 3. Action
Designed an interactive **Excel Dashboard** using Slicers for Age Group and Rating segmentation.
* **Volume vs. Quality:** Utilized Combo Charts to compare review volume against average ratings.
* **Ranking:** Used bar charts to rank product classes by positive feedback frequency.
## 4. Results & Insights
* **Overall Sentiment:** The analysis showed a strong **82.2% recommendation rate** across the store.
* **Demographic Insight:** While the **30-44 age group** drove the highest sales volume, the **60-74 demographic** was the most satisfied, with an **85.3% recommendation rate**.
## 5. Recommendation
* **Social Proof:** Leverage reviews from the **60-74 age group** in marketing materials to build trust, as they are the most enthusiastic brand advocates.
* **Inventory Targeting:** Target the **Tops category** inventory toward the 30-44 demographic to maximize sales volume where demand is highest.
